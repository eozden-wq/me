Fountain Pens

I really like fountain pens

You may be thinking, why fountain pens? The answer is really simple. Nothing goes beyond the satisfaction of sitting at a table, posture terrible, pulling out your overpriced antique pen, and writing a journal entryr

Some Popular Fountain Pens:

  • The Lamy Safari

    Pioneers in the fountain pen community, making the first mass-produced fountain pen, with a unique grip and a resistant, high gloss plastic body. A culture classic

  • Mont Blanc Meisterstück

    An expensive specimen of a fountain pen, for people with pockets deeper than anything they'll ever write using this. It's gold nib and iconic look doesn't leave much to be desired (other than an entire downpayment for a house-sized hole in your wallet)

  • The Pilot Kakuno

    This iconic pen from an iconic brand provides unparalled style and comfort, all at a very (relatively) affordable price. A newbie's best first fountain pen
